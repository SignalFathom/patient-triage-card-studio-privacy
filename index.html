Privacy Policy — Patient Triage Card Studio

Effective Date: October 21, 2025

Patient Triage Card Studio (“the App”), developed by Signal Fathom LLC, is a mobile application for emergency-management training and triage simulation.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not collect, transmit, sell, or monetize personal information to Signal Fathom LLC. All data you enter (such as names, triage details, or exercise notes) is stored locally on your device and within files you generate (e.g., PDFs).

Account Sign-In (Firebase Authentication): If you choose to sign in, we collect your email solely to authenticate your account through Firebase Authentication. We do not sell, share, or use this information for any other purpose.

2. Permissions

The App may request limited device permissions (such as file access) to generate and save PDFs. No information is sent to our servers.

3. Data Storage & Sharing

All files remain on your device unless you choose to share or back them up. Signal Fathom LLC does not access your data.

4. Security

Because data remains on your device, you control access. For training scenarios that include sensitive content, we recommend deleting files after exercises.

5. Children’s Privacy

The App is intended for adult training use and is not directed to children under 13.

7. Data Deletion

Users can request deletion of their Firebase account and associated data at any time by contacting SignalFathom@gmail.com. Upon request, user accounts will be permanently deleted from Firebase Authentication, and no user information is retained by Signal Fathom LLC.
